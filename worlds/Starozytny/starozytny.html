In a place more remote than the stars...

lies a world where magic is not a mere cheap trick, but rather something capable of making any dream come true... If you learn how it's done...

This world was created eons ago, long before the Gods themselves can remember. The how and the why were lost with the civilizations that shaped it, and the few who know keep the secret as if it were a precious treasure... All that is known is that those who made it are long gone.

And you may wonder: How is it that not even the Gods themselves know where they comes from? Legend has it that after the disappearance of a God, the new God who takes their place loses their memory, and a new era begins.

Although this is almost impossible to prove, the disappearance of entire cultures and their Gods has led to changes in pantheons all over the world. And what is most curious is that no God claims to be the creator; they simply occupy their throne in this age.

This has led many mortals to devote themselves to scholarship, trying to investigate the past, the origin of everything, the end of the ancestors, and the reason behind this strange cycle that does not seem natural.

But in the search for this primordial knowledge, they also uncovered other knowledge that should never have been revealed...

About 3,000 years ago, we have the first record of the arrival of the Interitus Rex, or the Demon King. Unfortunately, the text does not mention how or why he appeared, but almost every trace of the civilizations from that time was destroyed or on the verge of destruction.

Since then, every so often, a new Interitus Rex appears. It is not a cycle, nor does it follow any event; he simply appears to drown the world in blood... The last Demon King appeared 200 years ago, in the heart of the Karstnodian Empire, which had managed to unify almost the entire continent.
